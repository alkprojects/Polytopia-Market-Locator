Rules reference
Adjacency = 8 directions.
Windmill lvl = adj Farms (on Crop).
Forge lvl = adj Mines (on Ore).
Sawmill lvl = adj Lumber Huts (on Forest).
Market stars = min(Σ min(adj supplier lvl, 4), 8).
City territory = 5×5 around city (Chebyshev ≤ 2).
Ports do not feed markets in current rules.
Max 1 Market / Forge / Sawmill per city.
Territory = explicitly assigned tiles only, capped to city's 5×5.
Painting a City auto-assigns its full 5×5 (first city wins on overlap).
All techs & border growth assumed purchased.
